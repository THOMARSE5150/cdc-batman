About Celia Dunsmore

Celia Dunsmore

Registered Counsellor

Celia Dunsmore is a highly experienced counsellor with a passion for helping individuals navigate life's challenges with greater resilience and self-understanding. With over 15 years in clinical practice, Celia brings warmth, empathy, and cultural sensitivity to her work with clients from all walks of life.

Growing up in a multicultural environment, Celia developed a deep appreciation for diverse perspectives and the unique ways cultural background influences our experiences and relationships. This understanding informs her therapeutic approach, which emphasizes creating a safe, respectful space where clients can explore their concerns free from judgment.

Qualifications & Approach

  • Master's Degree in Counselling Psychology
  • Certified in Trauma-Informed Care
  • Specialized training in Cultural Competence
  • Member of the Australian Counselling Association

Celia draws from multiple therapeutic modalities, including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Mindfulness-Based Approaches, and Narrative Therapy. Her integrative approach allows her to tailor counselling sessions to each client's specific needs and preferences.

When not in clinical practice, Celia enjoys gardening, reading historical fiction, and volunteering with community organizations that support mental health awareness. She believes strongly in the importance of self-care and models this practice in her own life.
