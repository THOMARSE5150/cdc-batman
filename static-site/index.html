About Celia

With over 15 years of experience, Celia Dunsmore brings a wealth of knowledge and compassion to her counselling practice. She specializes in trauma-informed therapy, mindfulness-based approaches, and culturally-responsive counselling.

Celia is committed to creating a safe, non-judgmental space for all clients. She believes in the innate capacity for healing and growth that exists within each individual.
